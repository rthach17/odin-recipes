Kimchi Fried Rice

Description

A simple Korean-style dish using minced beef, rice, and of course, kimchi.

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons sesame oil
  • Â½ pound ground beef
  • 1 cup chopped kimchi
  • 4 cups cooked rice
  • 4 eggs

Steps

  1. Heat a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat and add sesame oil;
  2. Cook and stir ground beef until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes.
  3. Add kimchi; cook and stir for 10 minutes.
  4. Add rice; cook and stir until heated through, about 5 minutes.
  5. Move the fried rice to 1 side of wok.
  6. Crack eggs into wok and cook until bottoms are set, 2 to 3 minutes.
  7. Flip eggs and cook until set, 2 to 3 minutes.
  8. Serve 1 egg on top of each fried rice portion.
